Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to SKCS AI Sports Edge (Pty) Ltd ("SKCS", "we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (`https://skcs.co.za`) and use our AI sports analytics subscription service (the "Service").
This policy is designed to comply with the South African Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our international users.

2. Information We Collect
We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked to you. This includes:
- Account Data: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and account password.
- Financial Data: We do not collect or store full credit card numbers. All payments are processed securely by our third-party payment gateways (e.g., PayFast, Stripe, PayPal). We only receive payment confirmation tokens, billing addresses, and subscription status.
- Technical Data: We automatically collect IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, and device identifiers when you interact with our Service.
- Usage Data: We track how you interact with our platform, including which predictions (1X2, Accumulators, etc.) you view, to improve our AI models and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal data to:
- Provide, maintain, and manage your subscription access to the SKCS AI Pipeline.
- Process payments and prevent fraudulent transactions.
- Send you administrative emails, such as payment receipts, password resets, and subscription expiration notices.
- Improve our website, algorithms, and technical infrastructure.
- Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing (POPIA & GDPR)
We only process your personal data when we have a valid legal basis to do so:
- Contractual Necessity: To provide you with the Service you subscribed to.
- Legitimate Interests: To improve our AI pipeline, maintain platform security, and prevent fraud.
- Consent: When you opt-in to receive marketing communications (which you can opt out of at any time).
- Legal Obligation: To comply with South African company and tax laws.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties, marketing agencies, or sports betting operators. We only share your data with trusted third-party service providers necessary to operate our business:
- Payment Processors: To securely handle your subscription billing.
- Cloud Hosting Providers: To host our website and AI databases securely.
- Analytics Providers: To help us understand website traffic and usage trends.
All third-party providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and are required to keep your data secure.

6. International Data Transfers
Because SKCS AI Sports Edge operates globally, your data may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than South Africa (e.g., cloud servers located in the EU or US). When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, to ensure your data receives the same level of protection as required by POPIA and GDPR.

7. Data Retention
We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. If you delete your account, your personal data will be securely deleted or anonymised, unless we are legally required to keep it for tax and auditing purposes.

8. Your Data Privacy Rights
Under POPIA and GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- The Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- The Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
- The Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
- The Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to suspend the processing of your data.
- The Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- The Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your data for direct marketing purposes.
To exercise any of these rights, please email us at privacy@skcs.co.za. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Data Security
We have implemented robust technical and organisational security measures designed to protect your personal data from accidental loss, unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes SSL encryption, secure server hosting, and strict access controls for our staff. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit.
